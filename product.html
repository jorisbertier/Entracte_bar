Corona

PALE LAGER

Available

Corona beer is a widely recognized and popular Mexican beer brand that has gained global fame. It is known for its distinctive appearance with a clear, pale golden color that sparkles in the light. Corona is a type of pale lager, which means it has a light body and a relatively low level of bitterness compared to other beer styles.
One of the unique characteristics of Corona beer is the way it is traditionally served. It is often enjoyed with a slice of lime or lemon, which is placed in the neck of the bottle. This ritual of adding citrus to the beer is believed to enhance the flavor and provide a refreshing twist. Squeezing the lime or lemon into the beer can add a tangy and zesty note, balancing out the mild hop bitterness and contributing to its overall refreshing taste.
